Description

A NEW LANDMARK IN THE HEART OF PHNOM PENH

If you value quality, sustainability, and modern aesthetics, and desire to live in the heart of Phnom Penh, look no further than Odom Living Residence! Developed by Urban Living Solutions, the leading community-focused real estate developer in Cambodia, Odom is an exceptional mixed-use development situated on Norodom Boulevard. It comprises two towers dedicated to residential and commercial use.

The Odom project embodies the developer's commitment to sustainability and usability enhancements, embracing the concept of "work, play, and live." Odom Living, the residential tower within the project, offers remarkable aesthetics and spacious layouts that cater to the needs of contemporary urban families and young professionals seeking well-designed living spaces in the city center.

This 30-storey complex features 138 units, ranging from 78 to 262 square meters in net size. Each unit is equipped with high-end fixtures, fittings, ample natural light, and private balconies boasting stunning views of Phnom Penh's skyline. Residents also enjoy exclusive access to luxurious amenities such as a gym, swimming pool, rooftop terrace, green spaces, and a sky bridge connecting to the second tower, Odom Tower all included at no additional cost.

Odom's prime location grants convenient access to various attractions, including international schools, restaurants, cafes, shopping malls, banks, offices, and entertainment venues. Chip Mong Noro Mall is located across the road, while Aeon Mall 1, the Independence Monument, the Royal Palace, and the National Museum are all within a 5-minute reach.

With its outstanding features, Odom rightfully claims its place as Phnom Penh's future modern landmark. Inquire now to secure your place in this exceptional development!
